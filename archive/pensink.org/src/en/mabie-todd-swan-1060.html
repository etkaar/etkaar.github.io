Mabie Todd & Co. LTD

SWAN Leverless Pen – № 1060

Vintage Fountain Pen

The knob of this specific pen is not made of hard rubber – as usually in this model – but of celluloid; the cover of the pen cap in contrast is still made of hard rubber.

  • Origin — England, Great Britain
  • Produced — In the early 1940s (Estimation)
  • Type — Sacked pen with twist mechanism (early Swan Leverless system)
  • Nib — Gold nib with 14 carat
  • Works performed — Cleaned ▪ Superficially polished
  • Weight — 18 g
  • Length — 13.2 cm ▪ 12 cm uncapped ▪ 16.3 cm with cap on
